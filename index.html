With its stunning beaches, picturesque waterfalls, and rich cultural heritage, Misamis Occidental has become a popular destination for tourists looking to immerse themselves in natural beauty and learn about the local culture. A must-visit destination for tourists looking for a unique and diverse travel experience.

Jimenez Heritage House, a restored ancestral home that showcases the Spanish-influenced architecture of the area. With its intricate wooden carvings and colorful tiles, the house is a true masterpiece of craftsmanship.

Bawbawon Island and was immediately struck by its beauty and tranquility. The island is located off the coast of Plaridel, Misamis Occidental and is accessible by boat. From the moment I stepped foot on the island, I was greeted by soft white sand and crystal-clear waters.

The Subanen Village is also home to a variety of traditional crafts and artwork, such as woven baskets and intricate beadwork. Visitors can watch the artisans at work and even purchase some of their handcrafted items as a souvenir.

Tourists and locals can enjoy a breathtaking view of the mountains, Caluya Bay and Murcielagos Bay at Caluya Shrine. Besides the captivating view, visitors can also enjoy the fresh air.Theres a 45-feet high, huge statue of Jesus Christ, which is known as Christ the Redeemer, standing up on top of a hill.

Bless Amare Sunrise Resort or also Known as Bless Amare Sunrise Retreat Center And Resort is a beautiful white sand Beach Resort located in Brgy. Tugas, Baliangao, Misamis Occidental. Moreover, this Resort is known to own a long stretch of white sand shoreline comparing it to other white sand beach resort located in Baliangao, Misamis Occidental.

The first thing that catches most visitors attention upon arriving in Ozamiz City is the fort that stands guard over Panguil Bay. Dubbed as the Cotta Shrine or Fort Santiago, it is made from blocks of sedimentary rocks and coral stones found along the coastal parts of the city. It stands as one, and probably the sole reminder, of the citys once colorful past.

PIDUAN FALLS is a 60-meter high curtain-like falls located along the western section of Mt Malindang at Sitio Piduan in Barangay Napangan, Don Victoriano Chiongbian, Misamis Occidental. Experience untouched natural beauty amid the sound of crystal clear waters crashing into the rocks at the site that can be reached by a short trek along the riverbed, a perfect recipe for adventurers.
